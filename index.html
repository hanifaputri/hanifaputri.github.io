How to activate AR?

  1. Make sure that you already checked the "Allow for Camera" permission. Reload if the prompt is not showing.
  2. Point your camera towards the photo marked by the sticker. Please be mind that the sticker is just a helper sign, so don't forget to release it first, otherwise it won't work.
  3. Wait for a second and let the magic happens!
