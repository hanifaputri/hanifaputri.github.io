How to activate AR?

  1. Make sure you already checked the "Allow for Camera" permission. Reload the page if the prompt doesn't appear.
  2. Point your camera towards the photo marked by the sticker. Please be mind that the sticker is just a helper sign, so don't forget to release it first, otherwise it won't work.
  3. Click the "Play" button
  4. Wait for a second and let the magic happen!
